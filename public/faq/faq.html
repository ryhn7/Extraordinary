Frequently asked questions?

When and where will the main event be held?

The main event will be held on 19th of December 2022 at Radjawali Semarang Cultural Center.

Can I buy more than one ticket?

Yes! You can buy more than one ticket at a time in one transaction.

Can I book tickets for others?

Obviously! You can order tickets for other people in your transaction by filling in their personal data to get a different barcode.

How to exchange ticket for access to the main event?

After the transaction is complete, you will get a complete ticket via email that you must exchange with entry access on the D-day.
