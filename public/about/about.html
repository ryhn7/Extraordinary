The Theatre of Mariposa

Get Ready to Witness The Future

The odyssey of Mariposa proceeded through various stages. The first is the larval stage, during which the potential butterfly must live in restrictions, is unable to fly very far, and moves slowly. This represents our lives during the most recent covid pandemic, when there were many restrictions on everything. With The Theatre of Mariposa, TEDxUNDIP invites you to reflect on past occurrences that were full of tests as fodder for future lessons .
