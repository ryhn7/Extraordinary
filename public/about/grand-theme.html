TEDxUNDIP 3.0

The Meanings

  • Wise of Mind
  • The Multiverse of Disruption
  • Elevate Society 5.0

Wise of Mind

Reasonable mind and emotion mind combine to form wise mind.

The concept of a wise mind believes that everyone of us have natural awareness that can guide our actions and goals. It's a well-balanced, aware viewpoint that guides us toward our objectives by combining information and experience.

The Multiverse of Disruption

Everyone suddenly becomes "disruptive" and/or "innovation.

The pandemic's crisis is like adrenaline for innovation and disruption on multiple fronts, forcing impediments that would have taken years to overcome to vanish in days. But, from being a great concept when it was initially written, disruption has now become the whole mantra for every startup or digital company wanting to break through. To be able to rise from this epidemic and unpredictable moment.

Elevate Society 5.0

Integrated Society 5.0 to solve different societal concerns.

The realization of Society 5.0 aspires to establish a society in which innovative Industry 4.0 innovations are integrated into everyday life to solve different societal concerns. As a result, the society of the future will be one in which new values and services are constantly developed, resulting in more harmonious and sustainable human life.
